These exercises assume you have the pub.sql database schema and data in your local MySQL database

Exercise 1: Easy
Objective: Create an endpoint to filter authors by city and state.
example: GET /authors?city=Oakland&state=CA

Exercise 2: Medium
Objective: Create an endpoint to update a specific title's price 
	but only for a specific minimum price of 20.
PUT /titles/title_id?price=19

Exercise3: Create an Endpoint to List Employees and Their Job Descriptions
Objective: Create an Express endpoint that retrieves a list of all employees 
    along with their job descriptions using a JOIN between the employee and jobs tables.
